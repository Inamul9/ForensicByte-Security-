Bot Commands

Explore all the powerful commands available in QUICK MAXXX. From moderation to entertainment, we've got everything you need.

/ban Moderation
Ban a user from the server with optional duration and reason.
/ban user:@username duration:7d reason:Breaking rules
/ban @user /ban @user 24h /ban @user 7d Spam
/kick Moderation
Kick a user from the server. They can rejoin with an invite.
/kick user:@username reason:Optional reason
/kick @user /kick @user Being rude
/mute Moderation
Mute a user temporarily or permanently.
/mute user:@username duration:1h reason:Spam
/mute @user 30m /mute @user 2h Spam /mute @user permanent
/play Music
Play music from YouTube, Spotify, or SoundCloud.
/play query:song name or URL
/play despacito /play https://youtube.com/... /play spotify:track:...
/skip Music
Skip the current song in the queue.
/skip
/skip

/poll Fun & Games
Create an interactive poll with multiple options.
/poll question:What's your favorite color? options:Red,Blue,Green
/poll "Best game?" "Minecraft" "Fortnite" "Among Us" /poll "Pizza toppings?" "Pepperoni" "Cheese" "Veggie"
/roll Fun & Games
Roll dice with custom sides and quantities.
/roll dice:2d20
/roll 1d6 /roll 2d20 /roll 4d6
/8ball Fun & Games
Ask the magic 8-ball a question and get a random response.
/8ball question:Will I win the lottery?
/8ball "Am I awesome?" /8ball "Should I study?"
